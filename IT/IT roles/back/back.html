Responsibilities


Responsibilities of back-end Developer

Back End Development

  • To design effective and efficient solutions, they must first gain a thorough grasp of the website's performance demands and goals.
  • Create a website architecture by utilizing correct product lifecycle approaches, such as Agile Scrum and frameworks.
  • Develop data acceptance and storage solutions for websites, particularly for those involved in payment processing.

API Development and Administration

  • Application Programming Interfaces (APIs) development and administration.
  • Write, test, and maintain development solutions for code-related problems are all part of the job.
  • To identify new features, communicate effectively with developers, designers, and system administrators.

System Logic and Troubleshooting

  • Organize the system logic.
  • Provide remedies to difficulties with the system.
  • Debug and troubleshoot apps.

Who can apply for this role?

IT Degrees:

Computer Science (CS): Provides a strong foundation in programming languages, algorithms, data structures, databases, and software design principles.

Software Engineering (SE): Focuses on the practical application of software development principles.

Information Technology (IT): Offers a broader perspective on technology, including networking, systems administration, and security.

Information Systems (IS): Blends business and technology, focusing on how technology can be used to solve business problems.

Non-IT Degrees (with additional education/experience):

Mathematics: Valuable for data analysis and algorithm design.

Physics: Transferable analytical mind and problem-solving skills.

Statistics: Beneficial for data analysis and working with large datasets.

Business Administration (B.Com): Relevant with additional programming or web development courses.

Important Considerations:

Skills over Degrees: Strong programming skills, knowledge of relevant technologies, and a portfolio are crucial.

Self-Learning: Stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies through online courses, tutorials, and personal projects.
